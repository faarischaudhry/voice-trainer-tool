Built for Discovery Sessions
These aren't pitch practice scenarios — they're discovery sessions. Each customer withholds information until you ask the right questions. They reward good discovery behavior (open-ended questions, active listening, uncovering pain) and penalize premature pitching. The goal is to surface their real blockers, not to demo features they didn't ask about.

How It Works
01
🎭
Choose a customer
Each persona has a real company, real Oracle environment, and real internal pressure. Read their profile carefully — it tells you what they care about.
02
🔍
Run the discovery
Ask open-ended questions. Uncover their environment, pain, and blockers. They won't volunteer everything — you need to draw it out. Avoid pitching OCI until you understand their situation.
03
🎙️
Speak or type
Use your mic for realism or type your responses. The AI customer replies in character — including pushback, skepticism, and the occasional tangent.
04
📋
Get coached
After the session, review your transcript and receive AI coaching on your discovery technique — what worked, what didn't, and where you lost or gained trust.
